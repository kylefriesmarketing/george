# G FOR GEORGE — the tunnels of Stalag Luft III

**Play it: https://kylefriesmarketing.github.io/george/**

![G for George](assets/og.jpg)

Silesia, 1942–1945. Your Lancaster fell over Essen; you and your navigator are all that's left
of her crew. In the Luftwaffe's escape-proof camp, six hundred officers are digging three
tunnels at once — and the one under the stove in Hut 104 needs 336 feet.

The events are true. The names are changed. The last page has the real ones.

- **The tunnel is the meter** — the shelf's first *constructive* resource: watch the feet
  accumulate across a year of camp-time, trade HEAT against NERVE against KIT, and hold the
  thread with your navigator (THE CREW).
- **Fixed history, free thread** — Tom is found on schedule; the trap freezes on the night;
  the tunnel breaks ten feet short; the list grows for a week and stops at fifty. Where *your*
  number falls in it is yours to play.
- **Replay is retelling** — the whole story is told in 1994 by the last survivor at a reunion.
  Dark endings are the counterfactuals he still deals himself at 3 a.m. Witness every man's
  story across retellings to earn the true ending: **Answering the Roll**.
- 11 endings · 24 Mentions in Dispatches · the Log · the Kriegie Glossary · an afterword,
  **"The Fifty,"** that tells the true story straight.

Part of [THE SHELF](https://kylefriesmarketing.github.io/games/).

Built with vanilla JS — no frameworks, no build step. All music WebAudio synthesis;
scene paintings via gouache-style generation with full procedural SVG fallbacks.
Press `~` in-game for the lantern (debug), `m` mutes, `1–9` choose.
